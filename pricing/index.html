Why Micro Certifications?

Constantly changing trends in online education and a proliferation of different specializations make micro-certification a perfect solution for many different knowledge domains. MiCertify™ solves the problem of infrastructure by bringing test-taking to the cloud with all the bells-and-whistles of classic learning management systems (LMS), simplified to make the lives of academics easier, and schooling costs cheaper.

The rising cost of education, combined with the need for expensive infrastructure add burden to both institutions and students. Rapidly changing technology, combined with a wide variety of certification needs makes MiCertify™ valuable to individuals and organizations.
