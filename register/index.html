A Few Words About Us

MiCertify™ users are empowered to make or take tests, and share their results or keep them private. Before you sign up you may wish to read our privacy policy which assures you about your expectations when using our services. We take privacy, and our Terms of Service very seriously. It's our hope that this service remains a safe and vibrant community of enthusiastic members. Should you experience any issues, don't hesitate to contact us.

Take tests

Search and explore public tests, or sign up through your institution and enroll in paid platforms provided by fully vetted third-party content providers

Create tests in knowledge you are an expert in

Easily and effortlessly create simple tests that are graded on a percentage scale, and award a certificate of acheivement for those who complete them successfully. Optionally extend our platform with sandboxed Javascript to unlock the potential of interactive graphics as a teaching medium. Our platform inspires fairness but also is protected by an anti-cheat system.

Join the crowd

A key aspect of the MiCertify™ platform is the community interaction. Tests can be improved through crowdsourced responses, honing an existing test that benefits future students.
